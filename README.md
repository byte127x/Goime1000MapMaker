# Goime1000MapMaker
A cross-platform GUI map creator for Goime 1000 (https://github.com/carykh/Goime1000/)

<img width="1441" height="784" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bcc513ef-f015-42e6-a218-3d971c4c9eeb" />

Left-click and drag to place tiles
Middle-click and drag to pan around
Right-click and drag to fill tiles with air

Tile selection is at the bottom, and maps can be created, opened, saved, or exported as thumbnails in the menubar.
